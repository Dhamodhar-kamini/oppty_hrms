Leave & Attendance Policy

1. Policy Overview

This policy outlines the guidelines for employee leave to ensure a balance between personal well-being and business continuity.

Note: Weekends and Official Public Holidays are excluded from leave calculations and will not be deducted from your balance.

2.Monthly Leave Allowance

Employees are eligible for 2 paid leaves per calendar month.
(Subject to prior approval by the reporting manager).

3.Deduction Logic (Excessive Leave)

If leave utilization exceeds the 2-day allowance, the following deduction rules apply automatically:

  • Any leaves beyond the allowance result in Loss Of Pay (LOP).
  • The accumulated Paid Leave Balance cannot be applied to offset these specific deductions.
  • Calculation Multiplier: 1 Leave day = 1.5 Days Salary Deduction.

Calculation Example:
If an employee takes 3 leaves in a month, the system calculates: 3 × 1.5 = 4.5 Days Deduction.

4.Performance Implications

Consistent breach of the leave policy will trigger a performance review and may result in placement on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).

5.Sandwich Leave Rule

Leaves taken immediately before and after a public holiday may result in the holiday itself being counted as a leave day.
*Application of this rule is at management discretion.
