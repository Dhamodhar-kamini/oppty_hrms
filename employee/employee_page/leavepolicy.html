Leave Policy

The leave policy ensures a balance between employee welfare and operational efficiency. Weekends and company-declared holidays are not counted as leave days.

1) Monthly Leave Allowance

Employees may take up to 2 leaves in a calendar month without salary deduction, subject to approval by their reporting manager.

2) Leave Deduction Rule

If an employee takes more than 2 leaves in a calendar month:

  • All leaves will be treated as LOP.
  • First two leaves will also be converted to LOP.
  • Paid Leave Balance cannot be used.
  • Each leave = 1.5 days salary deduction.
  • Applies to total leaves taken in the month.
Leaves Taken Deduction
1 LeaveNo Deduction
2 LeavesNo Deduction
3 Leaves4.5 Days
4 Leaves6 Days
5 Leaves7.5 Days
Example:
3 leaves → 3 × 1.5 = 4.5 days deduction

3) Excessive Leave Policy

  • Employee will be placed under PIP.
  • Performance will be reviewed.
  • Possible actions:
    • Extension of probation
    • Role reassessment
    • Salary review
    • Termination

4) Sandwich Leave Policy

Leave before and after a holiday may cause the holiday to be counted as leave (management discretion).
